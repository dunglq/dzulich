The Verandah

 48, Oriental Ave, Bang Rak, Bangkok 10500, Thailand
 +66-26599000

Thông tin chung

The Verandah is an a place serving international cuisine where you will be offered everything right from delicious food, deserts to the spectacular river side view while you are dining.

The hotel is located on the side of River Chao Phraya and the Verandah is said to be one of the best options to take boats on the water. You will have an option to sit indoors or outdoors and chill-out. At night the place is very romantic as the terrace of The Verandah is lit up with numerous candles.
